Expert Commercial Roof Installation

Lutz Roofing Co., Inc. provides professional commercial roof installation services throughout the Mid-western United States. As authorized installers for leading roofing manufacturers, we ensure the highest quality installations backed by comprehensive warranties.

Our Installation Services

We specialize in the installation of all major commercial roofing systems, including:

  • EPDM (Rubber Roofing) - Ballasted, fully-adhered, and mechanically attached systems
  • TPO/PVC - Heat-welded white membrane systems for energy efficiency
  • Built-Up Roofing (BUR) - Traditional multi-layer tar and gravel systems
  • Modified Bitumen - APP and SBS modified systems
  • Metal Roofing - Standing seam and architectural metal installations
  • Green Roofs - Vegetative roof systems for sustainability
  • Roof Coatings - Protective coating systems to extend roof life

Our Installation Process

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your building and roofing needs, discussing options that best fit your budget and requirements.

2. Design & Planning

Our team develops detailed plans and specifications, coordinating with architects, engineers, and building owners as needed.

3. Professional Installation

Our experienced crews execute the installation with precision, following all manufacturer specifications and safety protocols.

4. Quality Inspection

Each installation is thoroughly inspected to ensure it meets our high standards and manufacturer requirements.

5. Warranty Activation

We complete all necessary documentation and activate your manufacturer warranty for long-term protection.
