Commercial Roof Maintenance

Preventive maintenance programs to extend roof life and reduce costs

Proactive Roof Maintenance Programs

A well-maintained commercial roof can last decades longer than a neglected one. Lutz Roofing's preventive maintenance programs are designed to protect your investment, extend your roof's lifespan, and prevent costly emergency repairs.

Why Roof Maintenance Matters

Regular maintenance is the most cost-effective way to manage your commercial roofing asset. Benefits include:

  • Extended roof lifespan - often by 10+ years
  • Prevention of major leaks and costly water damage
  • Lower long-term roofing costs
  • Maintained manufacturer warranties
  • Early detection of potential problems
  • Reduced energy costs through proper roof performance
  • Compliance with building codes and insurance requirements
  • Detailed documentation for asset management

Our Maintenance Services

Scheduled Roof Inspections

Comprehensive roof inspections conducted at scheduled intervals (typically twice per year - spring and fall). Our inspections include:

  • Complete roof surface examination
  • Drainage system evaluation
  • Flashing and penetration inspection
  • Membrane and seam assessment
  • Documentation with photos and detailed reports

Preventive Repairs

Minor repairs and maintenance tasks performed during inspections to prevent larger issues:

  • Debris removal and cleaning
  • Minor membrane repairs
  • Drain cleaning and clearing
  • Sealant replacement
  • Fastener tightening
  • Ponding water mitigation

Seasonal Maintenance

Seasonal preparation and maintenance to protect your roof year-round:

  • Fall: Debris removal, drain clearing, winter preparation
  • Spring: Post-winter inspection, damage assessment
  • Summer: Heat-related inspections, UV damage assessment
  • Storm response: Post-storm inspections and damage documentation

Documentation & Reporting

Complete records of all maintenance activities:

  • Detailed inspection reports with photos
  • Maintenance history tracking
  • Warranty documentation
  • Recommendations for future repairs or replacements
  • Digital record keeping for easy access

Maintenance Program Options

We offer flexible maintenance programs tailored to your needs:

Basic Program

Semi-annual inspections with detailed reporting and minor preventive maintenance included.

Comprehensive Program

Quarterly inspections, preventive repairs up to specified dollar amount, priority emergency service, and complete documentation.

Custom Programs

Tailored maintenance schedules for multi-property portfolios, specialized roofing systems, or specific building requirements.

What We Inspect

Our comprehensive inspections cover all critical roof components:

  • Roof Surface: Membrane condition, punctures, blisters, splits, and deterioration
  • Seams & Joints: Heat welds, adhesive seams, and all roof system connections
  • Flashing: Parapet walls, penetrations, edges, and transition areas
  • Drainage: Drains, scuppers, gutters, and downspouts
  • Penetrations: HVAC units, vents, pipes, and skylights
  • Structural: Deck condition, insulation, and structural supports
  • Accessories: Walkway pads, equipment supports, and safety equipment

Cost Savings Through Maintenance

Studies show that every dollar spent on preventive maintenance saves $4-5 in future repair costs. Regular maintenance:

  • Prevents small problems from becoming major failures
  • Avoids water damage to building interior and contents
  • Maintains energy efficiency
  • Extends roof life, delaying costly replacement
  • Reduces emergency repair calls
  • Maintains property value
