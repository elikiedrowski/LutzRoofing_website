Expert Commercial Roof Repair Services

When your commercial roof needs repairs, Lutz Roofing responds quickly with experienced crews and proven solutions. We understand that roof leaks and damage can disrupt your business operations, which is why we offer 24/7 emergency service throughout the Mid-western United States.

24/7 Emergency Roof Repairs

Roof emergencies don't wait for business hours. Our emergency response team is available around the clock to address urgent roofing issues including:

  • Active roof leaks and water infiltration
  • Storm damage assessment and temporary repairs
  • Wind damage and blown-off roofing materials
  • Emergency tarping and weather protection
  • Structural damage assessment
  • Water damage mitigation

Common Commercial Roof Repairs

Our experienced technicians can address all types of commercial roof repairs, including:

Leak Repairs

Expert diagnosis and permanent repair of roof leaks, including seam failures, punctures, and flashing issues.

Membrane Repairs

Patch and repair of damaged single-ply membranes (EPDM, TPO, PVC) including proper heat welding and sealing.

Flashing Repairs

Replacement and repair of roof flashing around penetrations, parapet walls, and roof edges.

Drainage Repairs

Repair or replacement of roof drains, scuppers, and downspouts to prevent ponding water.

Storm Damage

Assessment and repair of wind, hail, and weather-related damage to all roofing systems.

Structural Repairs

Repair of damaged roof decking, insulation, and structural components.

Our Repair Process

  1. Emergency Response - We respond quickly to your call, often within hours for emergency situations.
  2. Thorough Inspection - Our technicians perform a complete roof assessment to identify all issues and their root causes.

  4. Professional Repair - Our experienced crews complete repairs using quality materials and proven techniques.
  5. Quality Assurance - We inspect all completed work to ensure repairs meet our high standards.
